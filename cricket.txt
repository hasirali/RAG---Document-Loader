Under floodlights bright or skies so blue,
Twenty-two hearts chase dreams anew.
The bowler runs with fire and pace,
The batter stands with fearless grace.

The leather sings through summer air,
Crowds erupt from every chair.
A cover drive, so smooth, so clean,
Like poetry painted on the green.

From dusty gullies to stadium roars,
Cricket lives through open doors.
A child with bat and taped-up ball,
Still dreams the biggest dream of all.

Last over now, the tension grows,
Every heartbeat faster flows.
One shot flies beyond the line,
And strangers cheer as stars align.

More than a game of bat and spin,
It’s where passion lives within.
Through every loss and victory’s light,
Cricket keeps the soul alive each night.
